Welcome to Gate of Heaven AG Church

"A Place of Worship, Hope, and Transformation"

Gate of Heaven AG Church is a Christ-centered community where faith comes alive. Our mission is to share God’s love, inspire spiritual growth, and serve those in need.

  • Spirit-Filled Worship – Uplifting praise and worship that connects hearts to God.
  • Prayer & Healing Ministry – A place where prayers are heard, and miracles happen.
  • Youth & Family Focus – Helping families and young believers grow in faith.

Every service is an opportunity to grow spiritually, find encouragement, and connect with others who share your journey. From Sunday gatherings to small group meetings and outreach programs, there’s a place for you to belong.

Join Us!

Be part of a loving church family that welcomes everyone. Whether you’re new to faith or looking for a place to belong, there’s a home for you here.

Take the next step in your faith journey. Join us this Sunday and become part of something greater!
